Description

 

Study of advanced programming topics focused on logical structures of data as well as the design, implementation, and analysis of algorithms operating on these structures.

Topics include linked lists, stacks, trees, queues, graphs and analysis of efficiency. Also covers searching, sorting and hashing techniques.
